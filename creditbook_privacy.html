Privacy Policy

CreditBook App

Last updated: December 26, 2025

CreditBook ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that identifies, relates to, describes, references, is capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with a particular consumer or device.

a. Personal Data

  • Phone Number: We collect your phone number for account creation, authentication (via OTP), and identity verification.
  • Business Information: Name, business name, and business type to personalize your experience.
  • Contacts (Optional): With your permission, we may access your contacts to help you easily add customers to your ledger. This data is processed locally or securely transmitted to identifying customers.

b. Transaction Data

We collect details of the financial transactions you record (amounts, dates, customer names) to provide the core functionality of the digital ledger.

c. Device and Usage Data

We may automatically collect information about your device (model, OS version), IP address, and how you use the App to improve performance and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our App.
  • To verify your identity and prevent fraud.
  • To process your transactions and manage your digital ledger.
  • To improve, personalize, and expand our App's features.
  • To communicate with you regarding updates, security alerts, and support.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase services (Firestore, Auth). We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and secure access controls, to protect your personal information.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following customized circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share data with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., Google Firebase) necessary to operate the App.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access to your personal data.
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion of your account and personal data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.
