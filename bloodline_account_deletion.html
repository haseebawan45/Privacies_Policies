Account Deletion

How to delete your BloodLine account and associated data

Delete Your Account

You can delete your BloodLine account at any time. This action will permanently remove all your personal data from our servers.

How to Delete Your Account

1

Through the Mobile App

  1. Open the BloodLine app on your device
  2. Tap on the Profile icon (usually located in the bottom navigation or top-right corner)
  3. Tap on the Settings icon in your profile section
  4. Scroll down to the bottom of the Settings page
  5. Tap on the Data Deletion button
  6. Read the warning message and confirm your decision
  7. Enter your password to verify your identity
  8. Tap Delete My Account to complete the process

Navigation Path: Profile Icon → Settings Icon → Scroll to Bottom → Data Deletion Button

2

Contact Support

If you're unable to delete your account through the app, you can contact our support team:

  • Email: support@bloodlineapp.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Include: Your registered email address and reason for deletion

We will process your request within 48 hours.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Personal Information

Name, email, phone number, and profile details

Medical Data

Blood type, health status, and eligibility information

Donation History

Past donations, requests, and activity logs

Location Data

Saved addresses and location preferences

Preferences

Notification settings and app preferences

Communications

Messages, feedback, and support conversations

Important Information

Account Deletion is Permanent

Once your account is deleted, this action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 48 hours. Complete data removal may take up to 30 days.

Data Retention

Some anonymized data may be retained for legal compliance and safety purposes as outlined in our Privacy Policy.

Reactivation

You have 7 days to reactivate your account by logging in. After this period, deletion becomes permanent.

Alternatives to Account Deletion

Before deleting your account, consider these alternatives:

Deactivate Account

Temporarily disable your account while keeping your data safe

Disable Notifications

Turn off all notifications while keeping your account active

Privacy Settings

Adjust your privacy settings to limit data sharing

Need Help?

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance, our support team is here to help.
