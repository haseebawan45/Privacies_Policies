Warning: Deleting your account is permanent. All your data, including transaction history, customers, and business profile, will be erased and cannot be recovered.

To delete your account, you can either verify instantly through the app or submit a request below if you no longer have access to the app.

Option 1: Delete via App

  1. Open the CreditBook App.
  2. Go to Settings > Profile.
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.

Option 2: Submit a Request

Please provide the details associated with your account to initiate the deletion process.
